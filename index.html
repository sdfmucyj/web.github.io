Cell Suspension Culture Patent

The utility model discloses a cell hanging culture auxiliary device, which comprises a container and a semi-permeable film fixer. The semi-permeable film fixer comprises an inner ring body and an outer card part, the outer card part is arranged on a container, and the inner ring body and the outer card part are fixed in the container; The outer card is an iris opening and closing mechanism, and the semi-permeable film is reliably fixed with the inner ring body. The device provides a good cell culture environment to avoid the problem of poor simulation effect caused by cell sticking to the wall in the glass culture environment. In the device provided in this application, the cells contact the semi-permeable membrane, the contact area is smaller, and the state is suspended. The device is simple to disassemble and assemble, and can be used repeatedly; The fixing of the semi-permeable film is reliable, and the problem of contamination and slippage caused by incomplete fixing of the semi-permeable film is avoided.
